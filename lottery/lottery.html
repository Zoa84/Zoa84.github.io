Lottery

How to play:

  • Choose a bet amount
  • Pick your lottery numbers:
    • You can select a lottery ball by clicking on a ball, changing the colour to green
    • You can unselect a lottery ball by clicking it again, changing the colour back to red
    • Clicking 'Lucky Dip' will auto select your remaining lottery numbers
    • 'Reset' will unselect all currently selected lottery balls
  • Once you have chosen your numbers, and the 'Play!' button is green, click 'Play!' to roll the lottery!
  • The winning numbers will then be revealed, where a winning number which does not match turns blue, and a winning number which matches will turn gold!
  • Click 'Play again' to play another round!
  • Click any of the debug buttons to guarantee the chosen number of matches (This will click 'Play!' automatically)
