Employment

Games Tester at Rockstar Lincoln

I worked testing mostly Red Dead Redemption 2 on both Singleplayer and Multiplayer, testing both generally and on a focused area of the project. This also included travelling up to Rockstar North for training and testing, and working as part of a team in an area as well as separately depending on what is required.

Working here has given me a huge insight into the Games Industry, as well as the level of detail that Rockstar Games goes into in creating their games. I also experienced the crunch period coming up to the release of Red Dead Redemption 2, and having to manage my time between work and personal life, as well as managing what needed to be done at work.

Testing included working through a test plan when working through a new area, or when working through an existing area to find new bugs from the latest build, doing sweeps of specific areas especially if there were major changes made to those areas, and testing on different platforms and consoles.
