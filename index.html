The Website of Simon Chiu / Zoa84

Hello and welcome. My name is Simon Chiu, and I also go by the username Zoa84 for various other sites.

Senior Game Developer at Push Gaming, and former Principle Developer at Live 5 Ltd. I have worked on both frontend and backend creating slots games for desktop/mobile.

I am a Computer Games Programming graduate, graduating in 2017 at De Montfort University with a 1st Class Honours, and previously have worked at Rockstar Lincoln as a Games Tester.
